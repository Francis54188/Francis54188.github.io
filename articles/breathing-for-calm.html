Simple Breathing Exercises for Calm

When your mind feels busy or your heart feels heavy, your breath is always there to help you slow down. These simple exercises require nothing but your attention, and they work anywhere, anytime.

1. The 4-7-8 Technique

This gentle pattern helps activate your body’s relaxation response:

  • Breathe in slowly through your nose for 4 counts.
  • Hold your breath for 7 counts.
  • Exhale completely through pursed lips for 8 counts.
  • Repeat 4 times, letting each cycle feel softer than the last.

2. Box Breathing

A grounding exercise that brings rhythm back to your nervous system:

  • Inhale for 4 counts.
  • Hold for 4 counts.
  • Exhale for 4 counts.
  • Pause for 4 counts.
  • Continue for 3-5 full rounds.

You don’t need to “do it right” — you just need to show up for yourself. Even 1 minute of slow, intentional breathing can make a difference.
