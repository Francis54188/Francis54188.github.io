About Francis Mindful Cloud Healing

My Story

Hi, I'm Francis — an 18-year-old INTP boy who once fell into the deepest emotional abyss, and fought my way back to the light.

I once stepped into a top high school with great hopes, only to be swallowed by overwhelming pressure from competitions and academics. Depression and bipolar disorder took over my life. My logical mind, which I had always relied on, became completely helpless in the face of my out-of-control emotions. Every morning I woke up with only one thought: to end it all. I was trapped between the urge to destroy everything in my rage and the overwhelming numbness of wanting to do nothing at all.

At first, I let the darkness consume me. But with the support of my family, I began to fight back — through therapy, transcranial magnetic stimulation, and working with three counselors at once. Slowly, I learned to use my INTP rationality to untangle my emotions, and explored practices like meditation, manifestation, and metacognition to heal myself from the inside out. From being a passive patient to taking control of my mind, I clawed my way out of the abyss step by step.

Why I Created This Space

This is why I started "Mindful Cloud Healing" — to share the lessons I’ve learned, the coping tools that saved me, and the quiet hope that kept me going. Here, I write in English about emotional healing, self-care, and intentional growth, so that anyone who feels lost in the dark can find a little light in my words.

I believe that every wound can become a source of warmth for others. Thank you for being here, and for letting me walk this healing journey with you 💛
